Developed by Team Code Geass, Magnetic Sort is an experimental algorithm inspired by the behavior of magnetic fields. Elements are influenced by simulated forces—larger values repel leftward, smaller values rightward—until stability is achieved. It offers an engaging, physics-inspired approach to sorting, ideal for educational and visualization tools.
